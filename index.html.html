Md Reyad Hossain
Pabna, Bangladesh City of history & heritage

I am a dedicated BSc Nursing student at Rajshahi Medical University — a hard-working, self-motivated individual with strong communication skills, driven by a genuine passion for both healthcare and digital innovation.

Pabna — my hometown — is a city steeped in cultural richness, home to the world-renowned Hardinge Bridge, the historic Pabna Mental Hospital (one of South Asia's oldest), and the vibrant literary heritage of luminaries like Mir Mosharraf Hossain. This city shaped my values: resilience, empathy, and a relentless drive for growth.

Beyond clinical studies, I have cultivated a parallel expertise in digital marketing, social media management, and graphic design — believing that a modern healthcare professional must embrace both worlds.
